Fulfillment Service

Our e-Commerce Fulfillment service allows businesses to seamlessly integrate their e-commerce store and ship merchandise to our facilities and let us take care of the rest!

  • Specialty: Entrepreneurs and businesses with products who want to settle in the USA.
  • Convinience: Helping the modern entrepreneur bring their business to life hassle and worry free.
  • Practicality: Purpose-built solutions to help you be focused on your business; we can manage the rest.
  • Problem Solving: We bring your product to our facility and we pack it, send it, replenish inventory, and give you access to our platform feature.

    • How it works

    It's all done in 4 EASY steps:

      1. Connect your store and send us your products: Integration with our system and platform
      2. We receive, scan and store your products in our warehouse tracking your inventory
      3. Customers place orders and the orders begin to flow into our portal
      4. We pick, pack, ship to your customer
      5. We manage your returns and exchanges through our portal
